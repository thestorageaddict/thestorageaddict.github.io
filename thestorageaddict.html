 How long does delivery take?

All orders within Semenanjung Malaysia, Sabah and Sarawak are shipped using Poslaju. Poslaju is Malaysia's Express Mail Service and delivers within 2 - 5 business days. Signature will be required when you received the parcel. Tracking numbers will also be provided after we have sent your parcel.

All international orders will be shipped using Poslaju Flexipack International. Shipping take within 4 - 11 business days depend on your location. Signature will be required when you received the parcel. Tracking numbers will also be provided after we have sent your parcel.
